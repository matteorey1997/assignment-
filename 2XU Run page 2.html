Race Pack collection
Date of collection and location to be announce!

  • Things to bring:
    1. A photo ID (NRIC/ FIN/ Driver's License/ Passport) for the collection of Race Entry Packs.
      -It is mandatory for all participants to collect their Race Entry Packs within the dates stated above.
      -The Organizer reserves the right to refuse participants without the required documents from collecting their entry packs.

  • FOR PARTICIPANTS WHO ARE UNABLE TO COLLECT IN PERSON
    • -If you are unable to collect your race entry pack personally during the assigned collection days, you may authorise someone to collect it for you.
    • A copy of the runner's identification card and confirmation slip for verification
    • Your representative’s identification card for recording purposes No authorization form is required. Your representative’s particulars will be taken down for record purposes.

  • FOR FOREIGN PARTICIPANTS
    • Travelling from overseas for the race? We recommend that you authorise someone to collect it for you at the stipulated dates above. Otherwise, separate collection arrangements will be made for you. More details will be released closer to the event date.

Entitlement

  2. Finisher Shirt for those that complete 21.1km
  3. Finisher Medal
